What we do

In Ghanaian culture, frequently, children are given a "day name" which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Once you key in your birthday in the format :

  • Century digits (The first two digits in the year you were born) eg. If you were born in 1989, you key in 19.
  • Year digits (The last two digits in the year you were born) eg. If you were born in 1989, you key in 89.
  • Month digits (The month you were born in digits) eg. If you were born in June, you key in 6.
  • Day of the month (The day of the month you were born) eg. If you were born on 16th June 1989, you key in 16.
The app takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born. There are different male and female Akan names. Therefore, the name you get is determined by the day you were born and your gender.
